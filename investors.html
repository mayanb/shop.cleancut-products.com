Meet Our Investors

Learn more about our world-class team of investors

Brad Anderson
Brad has worked in venture capital for the past 16 years. He often looks outside Silicon Valley for undiscovered, product-focused founders with a drive to reinvent, rather than copy. Brad has made investments in startups across the United States, with a special focus on fintech, food-tech, and other cutting-edge sectors.

Yelena Vladimirskaya
Yelena is a former entrepreneur and CEO with deep expertise in the direct to consumer space within Russia. She has served on the board of several major Russian firms and spearheaded government initiatives to foster innovation. Yelena is also a frequent speaker at entrepreneurial events and workshops for women in entrepreneurship.

Robert Miller
Robert has always been a maker. He started his first company as a 16-year-old growing up in Minnesota, and spent the early part of his career as a serial entrepreneur. Drawing on his entrepreneurial background, Robert backs companies that use technology and design to build revolutionary user experiences.
